Books and the Human

Books and The Human is the result of a collaboration between the AHRC, Arts and Humanities Research Council and Central Saint Martins - part of the AHRC’s 10th Anniversary Debate series, entitled ‘The Way We Live Now’.

The project was the result of work done across a varied range of roles: exhibition design, curation, identity, letterpress printing, typesetting and bookmaking. Being involved in the discussions since early stages also made it possible to propose names for speakers, satellite activities, and marketing strategy.

In collaboration with Dr. Sheena Calvert and Shuruti Vengatesh. Photography by Gaetan Bernede.
