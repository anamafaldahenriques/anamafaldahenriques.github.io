Ana is a communication designer and bibliophile based in London. Her work ranges from editorial and graphic design, illustration and identity.

She has recently completed with distinction a Masters of Arts in Communication Design at University of the Arts London, Central Saint Martins (2016) and holds also a BA Degree in Design from IADE Creative University, Portugal.

Ana has worked for RMIDS Information Design Studio and with the Arts and Humanities Research Council for ‘Books and the Human’. She is currently working independently.

For further details or exciting collaborations, please get in touch, anamafaldahenriques@icloud.com
